I-Belong

One of the first places to check for fun is the I-belong site. They even have their own app that notifies you of upcoming events along with a calendar. There are several activities to choose from right on campus!

ORC

Love to get outside and enjoy the outdoors? ORC is just the place! They rent crazy cheap prices to anyone and recommend several great areas of recreation. They work just beneath the football seats!

The Tavern

Want to show off some classic country dance moves but BYU-I is full and only throws down on Wednesday? Come on down to the Tavern! They host country dancing nights almost each day for only $3 a person! They have some crazy dance moves that often aren't allowed on BYU-I grounds.

The Dunes

The St. Anthony Sand Dunes are a common hot spot for people to come together at night and host a bond fire. Gather some friends around to roast smores or dirt bike if you have one. When it snows it's a great spot to go sledding!

Madison Library District

For someone who loves reading this place is a wonderful oasis from the universtiy. This lovely public library is free for all and you only need an I.D and proof of address in order to obtain a library card. They have a decent selection of books that range from several topics. They also have a lil bookstore with discounts and programs aimed toward various ages. You can also donate your own books to the store. There is also a printing service for a fee however their computers and wifi are completely free!

Gravity Factory

When was the last time you were able to jump around on a trampoline and embrace the thrill of air? Plunge back into your childhood for an hour at the Gravity Factory! Here there are several trampolines and courses to explore! Bring a group of friends to let out some energy and tensed up muscles!

The Rock Gym

Want to climb up some rocks and walls but it's too cold outside? The Rock Gym has exactly what you need! Just beside Gravity Factory this gym is open to all ages and hosts several events throughout the year alongside promotions and discounts!

If none of these categorizes are what you're looking for then I suggest trying out the search bar on BYU-I's own website. I've used it a lot myself and it'll help you find several links!
