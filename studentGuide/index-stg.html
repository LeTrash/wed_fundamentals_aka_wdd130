Welcome to the BYU-I Student Guide! This is not an offical BYU-I website, rather it is an independant project from a helpful programmer. I've noticed that several students don't realize the amount of resources this universtiy provides. This site organizes those resources into a simple category system and presents a quick summary on the topic. I have expereince with a few of these links and they've been able to make my semesters 10 times better than I expected. I hope that you will be able to improve your life as I have been able to.

The topics below are clickable and will lead you to a page that expands on their category. Each item will have a little description and if you click on their title then it will send you do their site.
