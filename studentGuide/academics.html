Student Records

Having trouble with classes? Transcript problems? Make an appointment with the Student Records Center!

Academic Petition

Did a traumatic event occur to you during the semester? Were you unable to drop out of classes or inform your teachers of your situation? Submit an academic petition and you can get those W's cleared from your transcript.

McKay Library

The campus library is a huge resource avaiable to all students and friends. There are circulation desks on each floor to clear up confusion and check out books as well as printers that only charge $0.04/page Black & White or $0.25/page for colored. There are libirarins that specicilize in each department and are happy to help with research papers, sources, and access to materials. The Mab Lab on the first floor provides free access to adobe creative cloud, equipment for checkout, and 3D printing for only $0.10 a gram! On the 2nd floor there is also the Grandin Press which is a fully funcitonal repilic of the printing press that printed the Book of Mormon along side the Special COllections room that allow students access to primary resouce documents and prehistoric artificats. The second floor is also a quite area making it a wonderful place to study or complete assignments without disruption.
