Family Crisis Center

Located just by the Romance Theater on Main St. This lovely organization provides several resources for anyone in need. They provide amble Finanical aid as well as host a wonderful Thrift Store in the basement that stays open till 3pm and has weekly deals and items. There is also a food pantry in the back that is open to all.

ONESTOP

The BYU-I ONESTOP is a portal for 24/7 questions, latest campus information, and step-by-step tutortials. They have specific categories and articles to anything you'd need for campus. If you think your question is too detailed or weird to ask you can submit a personal request.

Financial Aid

Located in the Kimball building, this office provides help to all of it's students. They provide pell grants and student loans and can answer any question you have. They can even help walk you through the applications step by step. They take into account FAFSA but if you weren't able to complete it or aren't qualified then you can get tutiuon depending on your current GPA.

Medicaid

Don't have parent insurance to fall back on? Don't want to pay for the Universities own insurance? Apply for medicaid! The Idaho Department of Health & Welfare is the Division of Medicaid and they are open to students! It's easy to apply, either submit your information online or go in person! You may be denyed but you never know unless you try. I was able to get approved within minutes and I thought I wouldn't have a chance.

If none of these categorizes are what you're looking for then I suggest trying out the search bar on BYU-I's own website. I've used it a lot myself and it'll help you find several links!
